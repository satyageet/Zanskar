We don’t promise 5-star facilities, we deliver better, more than enough to cater to your day to day needs & make you feel right at home.

Facility

GYM

Stocked with an array of equipment ranging from the mechanically-operated Pec Deck machine for chest workout to the basic treadmill, from multiple dumbells to yoga mats, the Zanskar gym is an ideal place for all the fitness freaks. A lot of new equipment has recently been installed-a pullup bar, battle ropes, speakers, motivational posters etc, one could say that the gym has finally been brought in ‘shape’.

OAT

Being the only hostel in campus boasting of an Open Air Theatre, it is a frequent ground for various events ranging from movie screenings to watching live match broadcasts. Moreover, it is a friendly haven to relax and grab a whiff of fresh air after, say, a bad or tiring day at the institute.

Zanskar Innovation Centre

A great place for students to meet, discuss and plan start-up ideas, and thus help in the incubation of early age start-ups. We have also procured a funding of Rs. 20000 from IITD Alumni Association. As a part of agreement, we have also ensured that students will be given mentorship from successful alumni of IIT-D.

Central Living Area

Situated near the entrace of the house, this area is equipped with a TV and has a wonderful wall painting. Informative news and sport matches are played on the TV, creating a lively environment and providing a common platform for all residents to interact.

Visitors' Room

Lavish sofas, proper carpeting, a TV, wall painting are some of the exquisite features of the newly constructed room which feels more like a 5-star hotel lounge. This room has been built this year and definitely, it is the best visitors' room in the campus.

Cricket Nets

A new cricket mat has been installed and the condition of cricket nets improved significantly, so much so that you can often see players from other hostels coming for practice.

Dance Room

Adequately mirrored and ventilated, the dance room is an awesome space to get that body moving and grooving to beats bound to get you in a happy-go-lucky spirit while you are in there. We are the only hostel with a PVC-floored dance room and it has recently been renovated with new mirrors and curtains. It is extensively used during periods near institute dance competitions like the GD, IDP, and Duo+.

Basement (Indoor Sports)

The well-lit and ventilated basement of the hostel if well facilitated with equipment for indoor sports, viz. table tennis, chess, carrom, darts and pool. The boards, sticks, racquets, etc. are well maintained and are always ready to be used in good condition. This area is very lively during Zest, the Zanskar Sports Fest.

Pool Room

The pool room is appropriately furnished with an all new easy to grip pool table cloth. Situated adjacent to the TV room, it is a good place to take a recreational break during the intense routine of IIT. Due to the well-maintained pool room, Zanskar house has been retaining podium finishes in pool and snooker events.

TV Room

The TV room is in conjunction and at the same time, properly separated from the pool room to allow for no noise entering the adjacent facility. The room is well furnished with sofas and chairs. You will find this place crowded during the IPL and the UCL/El-classico fixtures.

Outdoor Recreational Area

After a tiring day at the institute, while you can make yourself comfy with the bean bags available at the entrance to the hostel, the swings outside make for a pleasurable joy ride. Hanging tree plantations have also been set up for good vibes. Heftly horticulture budget was passed this year to improve the garden area as well.

Night Mess

Opens at around 9 o’clock and remains functional till 4 in the morning. With an excellent delivery speed, the quality of food here is pretty appreciable. You have to give a try to the rolls they serve here. And if you’re not feeling like travelling the distance to their stand, fret not! They’ll deliver it to you at a minimal cost.

Juice Centre

Set just next to the night mess, it opens for a 24x7 period and serves a wide variety of fresh fruit chaats, fruit juices, shakes, tea and coffee. Moreover, the juice walah is adorably friendly and you’ll find it engaging listening to the stories he has for his customers.

Reading Room

Located in the basement of the hostel, the reading room is well separated, spacious and quite quiet. Open over a 24x7 period, it is easily accessible to the hostel residents and is heavily swarmed by the so called ‘maggu’ IITians pre-minors/majors. New bean bags and cushion chairs have been installed for the comfort of the residents.

Laundry Service

Although the washerman visits the hostel thrice a week, each floor is equipped with two washing machines available for use throughout the day and maintained in well working conditions. 6 new fully-automated washing machines have been recently installed. A washing machine user guide has been installed for the residents to acquaint themselves with its functioning.
