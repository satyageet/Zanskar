Zanskar House

As majestic as the humongous mountains we are named after, we are serving as a home away from home for the arguably the most brilliant brains of India since our establishment in 2006. Zanskar offers every esteemed member, a unique environment to develop and hone his skills to come off as a leader and successful individual, with amenities of great infrastructure and a family of students who are aces in a wide array of fields.

From academics to sports to cultural activities, Zanskar is a hub to discover your talent and explore your hobbies. Just like copper, after which the original Zanskar valley is named, we transmit a sense of pride and the skills coexistence and mutual benefits to live up to the virtue of “Win One, Win All”.

We not only give you a legacy to live up to but a chance to create one for yourself. So sit tight and enjoy your time with us, chill, make a new family, share your sorrows and happiness, to create memories and live the most memorable years of your life. And allow us to let you in on a small secret, It’s not just our hostel, it’s our home!
